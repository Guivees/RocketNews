YOUR CHARACTER IS LOOKING FOR A NEW JOB

For this assignment, you must choose a character from the book Les Misérables and write down the soft and hard skills s/he possesses. After doing this, you need to find, online, a job vacancy in English that suits these skills. Save the link of the job opportunity of your choice and don’t forget to take a screenshot as well.

Present Continuous

We use this tense to talk about actions that are ongoing (happening as we speak or “right now”). This tense requires the use of the verb to be (am, is or are) + main verb ending in —ing.
