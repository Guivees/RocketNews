Museum Security Guard

General Location: New York City, NY
American museum of natural history
Salary Range: $30,000 - $50,000 per year

Security and Surveillance: Proficient in monitoring and maintaining security systems, including CCTV cameras, alarms, and access control systems.

Emergency Response: Knowledge of emergency protocols and the ability to respond quickly and effectively in crisis situations, including evacuations and first aid.

Attention to Detail: Keen observation skills to detect and report any suspicious activities, potential threats, or safety hazards within the museum premises.

Communication Skills: Excellent verbal and written communication skills to interact with visitors, colleagues, and law enforcement personnel professionally and courteously.

Customer Service: Ability to provide a welcoming and helpful environment for visitors by assisting with inquiries, providing directions, and ensuring a positive visitor experience.
